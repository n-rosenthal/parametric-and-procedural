* ~parametric-and-procedural~
Study project exploring the generation and evolution of parametric and/org procedural methods for models, textures, and animations. The project is focused on the development of algorithms and techniques that can be used to create complex and dynamic visual content in a variety of applications, including computer graphics, game design, and virtual reality. The goal is to provide a comprehensive understanding of the principles and practices involved in parametric and procedural generation, as well as to explore new approaches and innovations in this field.
